"BOUQUET DE FLEURS"

May 25, 2024.

ASSISTED BY LIL SIS

I decided to take photos on 35 milimeter on my Nikon FM2. I took photos aswell a video to see what I can cook with in post-production. For photos, I set up a slow shutter speed to capture the whole flow of the movements, and it came out magnificent. Why the name "BOUQUET DE FLEURS" (bouquet of flowers in french)?. Simply because when I looked at this photograph, visually it was shaped like a bouquet, and accidentally it offers a visual double entendre on the embellishing beauty of Ballet. Assisted by my little sister Chaeyu Jung. Love.
