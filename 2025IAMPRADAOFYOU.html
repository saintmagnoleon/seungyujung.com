I AM PRADA OF YOU!

This was the second and last photo project I did before going back to Paris. I called Atila to come with his blue Volkswagen Touran 2006, as well as my homie Tukso.
Always proud of what I showcase to the public. People might not understand it right now. But it’s okay. It’s not for them, it’s for me. This whole first issue has been a sort of a public manifesto. The trajectory to make this magazine to life has not been linear. No work with intention has a linear trajectory. These last few months, there’s been: long breaks, times where I distracted myself, times of self-doubt, and times where I suddenly had a huge wave of ideas and had to seize the right one among hundreds... And all of that is done, while i am conscious. It’s a blessing. Brick by brick. WIC ain’t for the weak.
