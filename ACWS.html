A Conversation with Santiago

May 10, 2024. 17:40.

the moment i realized what i made, i knew i am going to be the next goat. TRUST. FAITH.

From working together with "THE BARRACKS", I thought it was a nice way to finalize it with a conversation recorded with my iPhone. Just a casual conversation where I tried to dig deep into things that could spark even more interesting topics to cover about during the conversation. I don't want to make it seem like an interview, I just want to let it flow naturally. I took my film camera with me but this time i decided to take photos between takes with my phone as well. I just put the default camera app on RAW and shot some pictures, and later colorgraded them. I was and I'm still unsure of how I was able to accentuate the right colors. The whole decor was already like this, i was photoshooting him in his actual room. Once I saw the coach next to the window, it was a wrap; natural light came through the window easily. Absolute cinemaaaaaaaaa.
